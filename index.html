FISH TANK

A CLAPWORKS PRODUCTION

How To Play:

Your goal is to keep all your fish alive.

Your fish will periodically get hungry.

If you don't feed your fish fast enough, it dies!

Your fish will earn you money if you keep them alive, which you'll need to buy more fish food.

Click on a hungry fish to feed it and keep it alive.

You need at least 1 fish food to feed your fish.

The game ends when all your fish are dead. You can buy more fish during the game, if you have the money.
